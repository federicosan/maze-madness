About the Game

Instructions

There are several sprites on the screen. The yellow one is your player and the red ones are other players. Use the Arrow keys (->, <-, etc) to move around in the maze and try to reach the ladder which is the end of the maze.

Power-Ups

There are two power-ups, the boostpad and a warp vial. The boostpad will speed up the player for seven seconds and the warp vial will teleport the player past a wall when 'x' is pressed.

Scoreboard/Chat

There is a scoreboard that keeps track of the most recent wins, shows the high scores and contains the number of warp vials possessed and whether boost is active or not. In addition there is a group chat to the left of the game that you can use to communicate amongst other Players
